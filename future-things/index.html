The Thing From The Future

WHAT IS IT?

The Thing From The Future is a game created by Stewart Candy and Jeff Watson at Situation Labs. The idea is to use the cards to generate the most interesting, funny or thought-provoking ideas for artifacts from the future.

When everyone is ready, share your future thing ideas with the group. You can share your favorite ideas with the gameplay community on Twitter using the hashtag #FutureThing.

READY TO PLAY?

Use the 'Shuffle' button to deal yourself a hand of 4 cards, one from each of the suits - Arc, Terrain, Object and Mood. These set the scene for you to create a brief description (and a sketch if you wish) of your thing from the future. This gets easier as your imagination warms up!

ARC is the crucial suit; it describes the type of future world that the “thing” comes from, and how far away it is from today.

  • Growth: progress has continued at something like its current pace.
  • Collapse: total breakdown. Society as we know it has come apart.
  • Discipline: order is deliberately coordinated or imposed.
  • Transformation: a profound historical evolution has occurred.

ARC

The type of future world that the “thing” comes from, and how far away it is from today.

TERRAIN

The thematic context or location where this object could be found in that future.

OBJECT

The focus for your imagination: a specific cultural artifact that reveals something about how this future is different from today.

MOOD

How it might feel to experience this thing from the future.
